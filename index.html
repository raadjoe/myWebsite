Assistant Professor (MCF) at the University of Paris-Saclay

Artificial Intelligence Reseacher at LISN

I'm a Software Engineer from the Antonine University in Beirut, with a Master of Research degree in Computer Science from the University of Burgundy in Dijon.

In October 2015, I started a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Paris-Saclay, and defended it in November 2018. During these three years, I had the opportunity to teach at the IUT of Orsay.

In October 2018, I started a 3-year postdoctoral fellowship at Vrije Universiteit in Amsterdam, working mainly in the CLARIAH project.

Since September 2021, I'm an assistant professor (maître de conférences) at the University of Paris-Saclay, conducting my research in AI at the LISN research unit.
